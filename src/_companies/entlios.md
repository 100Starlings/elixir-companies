Entelios enables large industrial energy consumers to commercialize their load flexibility into different energy markets in Europe, such as the classical demand response programs (PRL, SRL, etc.) or Intraday. Entelios' automated and distributed load control system is implemented in Erlang and Elixir. Entelios is based in Berlin and Munich, Germany.
